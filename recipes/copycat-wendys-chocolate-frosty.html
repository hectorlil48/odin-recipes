Copycat Wendy’s Chocolate Frosty

The Wendy’s Frosty is a cross between a milkshake and a soft-serve ice cream. This consistency is key and yet the hardest to mimic at home without a machine to keep it very very cold–our version is thinner, but it has the same satisfying fluffiness, mild chocolate flavor, and you can still enjoy it with a spoon. Plan ahead a little: Freeze the milk to get that thick creaminess—once frozen, the frosty comes together in just 5 minutes!

Ingredients

  • 2/3 c. whole milk, divided
  • 8 oz. Cool Whip, frozen
  • 2 Tbsp. sweetened condensed milk
  • 1 Tbsp. unsweetened cocoa powder

Steps

  1. Pour 1/3 cup milk into an ice cube tray and freeze until solid.
  2. Combine Cool Whip, frozen milk, remaining 1/3 cup milk, sweetened condensed milk, and cocoa powder in a blender and blend until smooth and thick.
  3. Pour into glasses and serve.
